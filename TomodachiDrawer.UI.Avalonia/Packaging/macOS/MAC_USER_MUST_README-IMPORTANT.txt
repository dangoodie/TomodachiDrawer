If the app doesn't open because macOS Gatekeeper says it's damaged or cannot be verified, you may need to do the following:

1. Open the Terminal app.
2. Type `xattr -cr ` (make sure to include the space at the end).
3. Drag the TomodachiDrawer.app into the Terminal window.
4. Press Enter.

After doing this, you should be able to open the app normally.
